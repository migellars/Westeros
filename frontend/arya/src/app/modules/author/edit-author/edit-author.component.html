The Citadel was granted to the Maesters in the Age of Heroes by King Urrigon Hightower, whose recently-deceased brother Peremore was a patron to the learned men. It is administered by the Conclave, the ruling council of archmaesters. The Citadel is also where young men from the entire continent are trained to become Maesters.
